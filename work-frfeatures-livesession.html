2021

Live classes are the core aspect of the FrontRow learning platform. The way online classes are conducted here is much different from usual video conferencing platform, with need of custom features and new methods. So we decided to build an in-house live class platform, on top of the 100ms live framework.

Need of a tool

To bring in a more interactive, rich and deep learning experience, the live classes on FrontRow demands custom features and tools, that are not readily available on the conventional platforms like Zoom, Google meet etc. These need to be designed and developed in-house, and integrated into an exisitng live video framework.
