A product video done for visualising E 2.0 battery by OLA Electric. The E 2.0 aka E2O is the new generation swappable batteries designed and manufactured by OLA Electric Mobility, which is the core of their EV ecosystem. A faster way to re-fuel electric vehicles such as E-rickshaws, E-bikes and E-scooters, compared to the conventional charging methods.
