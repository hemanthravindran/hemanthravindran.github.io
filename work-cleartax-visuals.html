Illustration guides for web

Clean, pleasant and organic shaped illustrations is the style that we follow

Fills

Pastel shades are used for a pleasant feel

Strokes

Darker shades from the fill palette are used for strokes

Color usage proportions

Extensive use of white and equal use of yellow and blue. Red and green are used as accents

Shading & Geometry

Friendly, organic forms are preferred over sharp geometry

Stroke style

Strokes are to be of varying width rather than equal width

Hero illustrations - Web

Hero illustrations are for banner sections in landing pages.
Colour proportions are carefully maintained to keep the visual balance
