Design System For Playlist Artworks

Visual Design | Algorithms

Designing a system that can automatically generate album covers for music playlists by taking in bulk data as inputs from excel sheets. Realised using design frameworks and algorithms. A project done for the music catalog team at OLA Play, which releases and updates hundreds of curated music playlists every month.

What to solve for?

1. Inconsistent Design

Design requirement given to multiple designers produce multiple outcomes. As a result there is no consistency among the artworks visually.

2. Zero Brand Presence

The entire catalog of playlists is basically categorized into two. Namely, OLAplay curated playlists and Apple music playlists. These need to be clearly distuinguished by the user without much thought. So there is a strong need for brand recall in the artworks.

3. Repetitive Manual Work

As said earlier, there are a hundreds of playlists being generated and updated every month based on the learnings from cutomers. But the creative team lacks bandwidth for such a tedious and repetetive task.

Solution : A 3-piece Design System

The Framework - For Consistency And Brand Presence.

A design framework is all that's needed to bring in consistency and brand presence throughout the artworks. By introducing such a framework, lot of manual thinking and creative effort can be avoided across the entire bunch of designers, thus reducing the manual work to some extend.

The System - To Bring in Automation

100s of curated playlists are released every 1 or 2 months in OLA Play which makes the job of a designer seriously hectic. The requirement given to the designer is a list of data like playlist title, playlist names, language name and related images. All these data are created by the catalog team and they pass it on to the designer to get the job done. Since there is already a framework which defines the dos and don'ts, why not create an automated system that takes the data inputs and generate album artworks, based on the framework. Thus the system takes care the job for the designer.

Hundreds of Artworks Generated...

Read More - Algorithm for Colour Selection

The most important aspect that's unique with every artwork is the combination of colours used in the graphical elements. This algorithm defines a systematic process for choosing the colours, by bringing in logical versions of the designer's intuitive thought processes.

Algorithm For Text Components

The Text components, namely Title and Name of the playlist are already defined with styles according to the framework. But to automatically adapt the text strings into the framework, is challenged by the number and length of the words in the input. This algorithm helps to maintain the design of text components in the framework, according to the dynamic text inputs.
