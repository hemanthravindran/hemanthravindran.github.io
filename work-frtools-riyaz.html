Practice with alankars

Alankars is a sequential arrangement of musical notes. Singing along with alankars helps user to calibrate their vocal notes. This tool makes the students sing along with an alankar and gives them realtime visual feedback so that they can perfect each notes

Listen mode :   Student start by listening to the notes which are played automatically

Singing mode :   Student starts singing along with the notes and tries to keep up with the visual feedback

Result :  Result is displayed as a match percentage of their vocal notes with the given notes

Identify and improve vocal range

Vocal range is the range of notes inside which someone can sing comfortably. There will be a lower end and a higher end, outside which a student can't sing. This tool helps students to identify their comfortable vocal range

The student is made to sing "Aaaa.." in their lowest and highest pitch possible one after the other

Lower and higher end of the range are identified when a note is sustained for more than 3 seconds

The range is identified as the difference between lower and higher ends. Comfort key is the middle point of the lower and higher end

Warmup with single notes

Warmup tool is a simpler version of alankars, where only one particular note is being practiced at a time. This helps in mastering different notes individually through constant practice

The lowest note the user can sing is first detected

Highest note is also detected similary
